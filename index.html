Hopscotch

A no-code analytics platform for user behavior analysis

Understand and optimize real user behavior. Hopscotch turns raw event logs into visual journeys, behavior patterns, and actionable insight — without SQL and engineering time.

Why it's different

Traditional funnels, cohorts, and dashboards, as well as user surveys, show only part of the story. Hopscotch reveals the full picture — how users actually navigate your product, where they explore, and why they drop off.

Key features

  • Visualize user paths to spot common journeys, drop‑offs, and bottlenecks in your product.
  • Explore event‑level data interactively and build behavioral segments.
  • Compare segments to highlight key differences in behavior.
  • Get AI‑assisted insights and explanations.
  • Optimized for working with large number of unique events.

Use cases

  • CJM exploration: describe how users behave in your product.
  • A/B deep‑dives: explain why the treatment group behaved differently than expected.
  • Root‑cause analysis: understand on a raw event level why a metric moved up or down.
  • Customer segmentation: group users based on their behavior patterns.

Ideal for

Product analysts, product managers, growth teams, and quantitative UX researchers – anyone who needs quick and deep behavioral insights.
