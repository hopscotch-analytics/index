Hopscotch

A no-code analytics platform for real user behavior

A no-code analytics platform that helps you understand and optimize user behavior through eventstream data. It automatically transforms raw event logs into visual user journeys and behavior patterns.

Why it's different

Traditional funnels, cohorts, and charts only show part of the story. Our platform reveals the full picture of user behavior — how users truly navigate your product, where they explore, and why they drop off.

Key features

  • Visualize user paths to identify common journeys, drop-offs, and bottlenecks in product.
  • Explore event-level data interactively and build behavioral segments.
  • Compare user segments revealing key differences in behavior.
  • Leverage AI-assisted insights.

Use cases

  • General CJM exploration. How to describe distinctive features of user behavior?
  • In-depth analysis of A/B experiment results. Why did users from the experimental group behave not as we expected?
  • Root cause analysis. Why did a metric went up or down?

Ideal for

Product analysts, product managers, growth teams, and UX researchers who want quick and deep behavioral insights.
